FinancePro User Guide

Overview – See total Income, Expenses, and Savings. Use the cards to jump into detailed views (click Income for Budget & Insights, Expenses for Transactions, Savings for Net Worth & Goals).

Transactions – Log every income and expense, search or filter by category, and export/import CSV for backups.

Budget & Insights – Track expense distribution, compare Budget vs Actual, and view a basic cashflow forecast plus your Financial Health Score.

Net Worth & Goals – Enter Assets and Liabilities to calculate Net Worth and see its trend. Create savings goals, then add contributions from your available savings.

Investments – Add assets with quantity and prices. The dashboard shows total invested, current value, and P&L. Selling logs income automatically.

Subscriptions – Track fixed monthly costs (rent, OTT, gym, etc.) and see your total monthly burn rate.

Quick Tools – Use the Tools button for EMI calculation, currency conversion, and a quick debt payoff tip.

Theme & Reports – Toggle light/dark mode from the header. Use Download Report to generate a PDF summary of your finances.

Tips – Start by adding income, then 3–5 expenses, set a monthly budget, and finally add at least one savings goal to unlock the most insights.
